Results

Model Results

Random forests provided the best classification for epilepsy. This method will give medical professionals another tool in their bag. Without this model, baseline costs would be around $16 billion next year. By improving diagnosis accuracy 20% those costs can be reduced by $3 billion. Getting patients into treatment sooner will increase life expectancy by seven years. Finally the load on the understaffed US healthcare industry can be shifted to higher yield strategies.
